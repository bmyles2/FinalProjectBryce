What Is Twitch?

Twitch is a live streaming platform that started launched in 2011, the platform was created for users are able to live stream activites that they enjoy doing such as cooking, 3d modeling, art and crafts, podcast, and many other activities, but the reason Twitch became so popular and what its most known for is the gaming that can be found on the platform.

Justin.TV to Twitch

Justin.tv was a livestreaming platform that was launched in 2007 as a live streaming platform for gaming, because live streaming gaming became more popular along with the website the creator launched a spin off of the site called Twitch.tv which gained millions of views and thousands of streamers, in 2014 Twitch.tv was acquired by Amazon.
